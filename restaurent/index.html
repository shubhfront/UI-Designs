Chef Arjun
Singh Rawat

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Trained at the Culinary Institute of America and with stints at Noma Copenhagen and The Bombay Canteen, Chef Arjun returned to Delhi with one obsession — to cook Indian food that feels both ancestral and alive. Every dish on our menu carries a memory, a technique, and a small act of rebellion.
