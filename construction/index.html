From Plot to Possession

A transparent, step-by-step process designed around you.

1

Site Visit & Assessment

We visit your plot, study orientation, soil, and surroundings.

2

Design & Planning

Architectural drawings, structural design, and 3D visualization delivered.

3

Contract & Approval

Transparent fixed-price contract. Municipal approvals handled by us.

4

Foundation & Structure

RCC framework built to IS code with premium TMT steel and M25 concrete.

5

Finishing & Interiors

Plaster, flooring, electrical, plumbing, tiling, paint — all coordinated.

6

Handover & Warranty

Final walkthrough, snagging list cleared, keys handed over with warranty documents.
